Our Story

A neighbourhood
café that earns
its place.

TinPot has been part of Fitzroy North since 1993. More than three decades of feeding the neighbourhood — the locals who've been coming since the beginning, and the new ones who stumble in and immediately understand why people keep coming back.

It's the kind of place where you settle in with a book, let the coffee arrive slowly, and forget what time it is. Mismatched furniture, Tiffany lamps, art on every surface, a fireplace in winter. A kitchen that takes its food seriously without taking itself too seriously.

Come in. Stay a while.
